AVI MetaEdit Help

About this Software

AVI MetaEdit was developed by the U.S. National Archives supported by Federal Agencies Digitization Guidelines Initiative and AudioVisual Preservation Solutions.

This tool permits embedding, editing, and exporting of metadata in AVI (Standard and OpenDML) files. This tool can also enforce file structure and metadata recommendations and specifications from U.S. National Archives, Microsoft, and IBM.
